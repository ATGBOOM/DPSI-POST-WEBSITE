In this article, I will be going through the various ways Artificial Intelligence and other aspects of modern day technology can have a rather significant impact on all aspects of heritage: an intriguing and surprising relationship. This link seems unbelievable but if used in the right manner, it can prove incredibly beneficial for both preserving our diverse past while keeping a modern touch, allowing for a greater understanding and connection. First, Artificial Intelligence and specific programs can be used to regenerate live experiences of the past through the medium of virtual reality. In fact, several museums make use of this brilliant idea, including the National Museum Of Finland. The experience recreates situations from the past to truly immerse one in the past, allowing them to live in the stone-age or the Renaissance. Moreover, online sites in many countries share the same purpose: to encourage tourists to explore the roots of an unknown nation in a familiar and relatable technology-driven manner. Artificial Intelligence can be used to decipher certain scripts of the past which get modern day scientists worked up. These scripts from the past further help us learn more about the ways of ancient civilisations and perhaps even redefine the history we have come to believe and accept. In 2019, Luo and a team of MIT Researchers made the headlines for developing an algorithm which could potentially align words from the lost language with their counterparts in the known language.
